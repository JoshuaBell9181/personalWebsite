More About Me

Before I realised that I was a Philomath, my teacher expressed that “there are three kinds of people in this world” he took a slight pause, “ones who can count and ones who cannot”. Fortunately, I am a person who can count. In fact, I am very good with numbers.

My name is Joshua Michael Bell, and I am a Software Engineering student at the University of New Brunswick. Like most 20-year-old males I enjoy socializing with friends, watching a good movie, and relaxing on the beach. Unlike most 20-year-olds, I have run a full marathon, jumped out of a perfectly good airplane, and went to Osheaga musical festival twice.

Although I do consider myself a thrill seeker, my most interesting quality is my curiosity to create. I love knowing how things work, that’s why I chose Software engineering. It’s cheap to build what ever you can imagine, you can develop very rapidly compared to real life prototyping. The best part is I have no idea what the future holds, but that I have the chance to create a positive impact on the world.
